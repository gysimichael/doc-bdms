Welcome to BDMS's documentation!
================================

BDMS (acronym of Borehole Data Management System) is a platform to manage data of soil investigations according to the `Borehole data model <https://www.geologieportal.ch/en/knowledge/lookup/data-models/borehole-data-model.html>`_ defined by the `Swiss Geological Survey at swisstopo <https://www.swisstopo.admin.ch/en/swisstopo/organisation/geologie.html>`_.

.. thumbnail:: images/overview.gif
    :width: 100%
    :align: center

    | **NOTE:** 
    | - The demo service is often reset and all entered data are deleted.
    | - The password is identic to the username: admin/admin
